Our society's emphasis on individualism urges us to become content creators in order to establish our uniqueness. The vast majority of images so produced become instantly obsolete as they land on the internet. Despite being intangible, this data has a physical cost as it occupies space on servers, ultimately contributing to ecological impact over time. Why not giving another visual life to these «digital wastes» by recycling them?

Select as many of your own pictures as you like, hover on the numbers and let them merge inside your browser. For performance reasons, each file should ideally weight under 1mb. The project can be found open source on Github.
